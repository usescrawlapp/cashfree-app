Refunds & Cancellations

Last updated: July 2026

1. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time by contacting us through our Contact Us page. Cancellation will stop any future billing, where applicable.

2. Refund Eligibility

Since this is a one-time ₹1 subscription charge, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Refund requests must be raised within 7 days of the transaction date.

3. How to Request a Refund

  • Email us at the address listed on our Contact Us page.
  • Include your registered name, email, and the approximate date/time of payment.
  • We will review and respond within 5-7 business days.

4. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method (UPI, card, or bank account) via Cashfree Payments, and typically reflect within 5-10 business days depending on your bank.

5. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in cases of fraudulent claims, incorrect information provided by the user, or where the service has already been fully availed as described at the time of purchase.

6. Contact

For refund or cancellation requests, please reach out via our Contact Us page.
